# Privacy Policy for WordFlip Language Apps

Effective date: June 4, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the WordFlip language learning apps by Azat Ismagilov, including Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, Italian, and Norwegian learning apps.

## Data Collection

The apps do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

The apps do not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, third-party tracking, or user accounts.

## Microphone and Speech Recognition

Some apps may request microphone or speech recognition permission when the user chooses pronunciation or speech practice features.

These permissions are used only to support the selected learning activity. The apps do not store voice recordings and do not use them for tracking or advertising.

## Notifications

The apps may offer optional local reminders for learning sessions. These reminders are generated locally on the user's device.

## Children

The apps are educational vocabulary tools and are not directed specifically at children.
