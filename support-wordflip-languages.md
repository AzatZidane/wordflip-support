# WordFlip Language Apps Support

Support page for WordFlip language learning apps by Azat Ismagilov.

Apps covered:

- WordFlip: Учи испанский
- WordFlip: Учи португальский
- WordFlip: Учи французский
- WordFlip: Учи немецкий
- WordFlip: Учи итальянский
- WordFlip: Учи норвежский

The apps are vocabulary trainers for Russian speakers. They work without account registration and do not require a paid subscription.

For support, contact:

- Email: ismagilowazaat@yandex.ru

When contacting support, please include the app name, iPhone model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.
